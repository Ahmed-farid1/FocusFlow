The Problem

Reading long assignments can be hard. Many students struggle to stay focused, especially when text feels dense or hard to follow. Students with dyslexia often find standard fonts and cramped layouts make reading even more tiring.

Most reading tools are either too complicated or not designed with real classroom use in mind.

Our Solution

FocusFlow is a simple workspace where you paste your text and turn on the reading tools you need — one at a time. No accounts, no setup, just paste and read.

We built it for hackathon speed: practical features that actually help during study sessions, not a full learning platform.

Features

Bionic Reading

Bolds the start of each word to guide your eyes through the text faster.

Dyslexia-Friendly Mode

OpenDyslexic font with adjustable size and line spacing for easier tracking.

Text-to-Speech

Listen to your text with play, pause, and stop controls.

Focus Mode

Hide extra clutter and increase spacing so you can concentrate.

Pomodoro Timer

25-minute focus sessions with start, pause, and reset.

Paste & Go

Copy text from a PDF, doc, or website and start reading right away.
